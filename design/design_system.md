# Neon Game Hub Design System

## Mood

Fun, futuristic, minimal arcade. The interface should feel premium and energetic without becoming visually noisy.

## Color Palette

- Background: `#07111F`
- Background Alt: `#0D1B32`
- Glass Surface: `rgba(255,255,255,0.08)`
- Primary Neon Cyan: `#28F2FF`
- Electric Blue: `#4C8DFF`
- Neon Purple: `#9D4DFF`
- Neon Pink: `#FF5CB8`
- Success Green: `#48F1A9`
- Highlight Yellow: `#FFD166`
- Main Text: `#F5F8FF`
- Secondary Text: `#9AB0D5`

## Typography Suggestions

- Headings: Orbitron, Bahnschrift SemiBold, or Exo 2 SemiBold
- Subheads: Rajdhani, Bahnschrift, or Eurostile-style families
- Body: Inter, Segoe UI, or IBM Plex Sans

## UI Principles

- Use rounded cards with a glassmorphism treatment.
- Keep depth subtle with soft shadows and layered gradients.
- Favor neon strokes and glow accents over heavy borders.
- Score bars and action rows should stay compact and readable.
- Animations should feel smooth and purposeful, not decorative noise.

## Screen Breakdown

### Home Screen

- Hero banner at the top with title, subtitle, and quick stats
- 2-3 column responsive game card grid
- Each card contains icon, title, short description, and hover depth

### Game Screen

- Top glass header with score, high score, pause, settings, and home
- Main play area centered in a painter-driven or widget-driven card
- Control buttons grouped beneath or beside the game surface

### Pause Menu

- Centered glass modal
- Resume, restart, settings, and home actions
- Short helper text so it feels like part of the app instead of a system dialog

### Settings

- Sound toggle
- Animation toggle
- Volume slider
- Font recommendations and future theme extension space
